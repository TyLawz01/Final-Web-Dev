How I Shoot a Game

  1. Pre-Game Routine
    • Charge batteries & format cards
    • Check weather and lighting conditions
    • Arrive at least 30 minutes early
  2. During the Game
    • Track the ball and anticipate plays
    • Look for emotion on the sidelines
    • Mix wide shots with tight action shots
  3. Post-Game Workflow
    • Back up files to an external drive
    • Cull favorites in Lightroom
    • Deliver a clean, edited gallery

Pro tip: remember at least one photo per player, not just the star of the game.
